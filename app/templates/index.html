6th Annual BLSJCL Certamen Tournament Coming Soon!

When and Where!

Saturday February 6th, 2016
9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.

Boston Latin School
79 Avenue Louis Pasteur
Boston MA, 02115

Basic Information

We will be hosting a certamen tournament (latin quiz bowl) with three levels of play: beginner, intermediate, and advanced.

Lunch and refreshments are included and all levels of skill are welcome.
Teachers and students, if you aren't playing, we welcome you to come and watch!

New this year!

This year, there will be workshops for teachers to learn more about Certamen.
Also, our very own Certamen players will be writing questions and doing behind-the-scenes work on tournament day.
